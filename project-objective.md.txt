# Autonomous Hiring Agent

## Project Vision

Build an enterprise-grade Autonomous Hiring Agent capable of automating the complete recruitment lifecycle using Agentic AI.

The platform should minimize manual recruiter effort while maintaining Human-in-the-Loop approval for all hiring decisions.

Unlike traditional ATS systems, this platform uses multiple autonomous AI agents that communicate, collaborate, verify information, and make recommendations before presenting results to recruiters.

---

# Objectives

The system should:

- Understand Job Descriptions
- Parse resumes
- Search candidates from multiple sources
- Rank candidates
- Schedule interviews
- Generate interview questions
- Conduct AI Interviews
- Evaluate candidates
- Generate hiring reports
- Assist recruiters in decision making

The AI should NEVER make final hiring decisions.

Final approval always belongs to Human Recruiters.

---

# Business Goals

- Reduce recruiter workload by 90%
- Improve hiring accuracy
- Standardize evaluation
- Remove manual resume screening
- Improve candidate experience
- Reduce hiring time
- Enable AI-driven recruiting
